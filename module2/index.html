Our Menu

Chicken

Chicken is a very tasty poultry and one of the most popular meats in the world due to its low price and ease of cooking. The meat itself is very versatile and can be cooked in may way including frying, baking and grilling.

Beef

This is another popular meat, but is less commonly found then chicken due to its higher cost. The meat is often eaten almost raw (or rare) and is commonly eaten in the form of steak.

Sushi

A dish very popular in Japan made of raw fish. These are often very carefully presented and are produced to be visually appealing. Generally quite expensive, the fish must be of the highest quality to avoid risks of getting ill from potential parasites that live in lower quality fish.
